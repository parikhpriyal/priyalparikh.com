EAi

Sr. Designer • EAI • 2017-2026

My journey at EAi Technologies began as a UI/UX Design Innovator, looking back, a title that set the tone for everything that followed. Over 8+ years, I grew from a UI designer into a Business Analyst and eventually a Project Manager, wearing multiple hats not out of necessity, but out of a genuine curiosity for how great products come together end to end.

In that time, I worked across 20+ projects for 13+ clients spanning industries as diverse as cybersecurity, healthcare, supply chain, logistics, and education. The work ranged from B2B portals and customer-facing platforms to cross-functional tools bridging internal and external teams; each one a different problem, a different user, and a different challenge to solve.

Beyond the craft, I built and led a team of 7, including developers and a QA resource. And hired and mentored 4 designers, helping shape not just the work, but the people doing it. Watching a mentee grow into a confident designer remains one of the most rewarding parts of those years.

I also participated in 4 company hackathons, exploring everything from cybersecurity and AI to game design, spaces where constraints sparked some of my most creative thinking.

But across all of it, the mission stayed the same. To design software that cuts through complexity — simplifying the long-winded tasks of operations teams, making large volumes of data consumable and actionable, replacing tangled Excel-based workflows with systems built for transparency and efficiency. And above all, to give every user, regardless of their role or industry, an experience that genuinely improves their quality of life at work.
