The person behind the work.

Based in Mumbai, I design at the intersection of physical and digital, crafting everyday experiences that feel both purposeful and alive.

I hold an MFA in Design & Technology from Parsons School of Design, New York; a program that sharpened my thinking around systems, interaction, and the human side of technology. I started out as a risk analyst, which gave me a deep appreciation for how products function at their core before I made the shift into interaction design in 2015. That cross-disciplinary background continues to influence how I approach every problem — with both analytical rigor and creative intuition.

Today, I lead design on a small founding team, working closely with product and engineering to shape experiences from the ground up. Before this, I spent 8 years at a boutique software development firm, where I honed my craft across a wide range of products and industries.
