Sales Portal

Lead Designer • Pitch Project • 2024

Managing warehouse sales across multiple locations, regions, and customers is rarely a simple task. Teams juggle inventory updates, open tickets, unresolved incidents, and regional calendars; often falling back on sprawling Excel sheets that were never built for the complexity they're asked to handle. As operations scale, these makeshift systems become bottlenecks, prone to error, difficult to share, and impossible to act on in real time. For decision-makers, getting a clear picture of performance across geographies, customers, or company-wide operations means hours of manual consolidation that could be spent acting on insights instead.

This sales portal was designed to change that. Conceived as a customer pitch, it set out to replace the patchwork of spreadsheets with a single, purpose-built platform to track warehouse sales and inventory, manage issues, and surface meaningful data across every level of the business.

At its core, the portal features a rich dashboard with a calendar view that maps warehouse activity across locations and geographical regions, factoring in public holidays for accurate planning. It surfaces key counts at a glance — open tickets, active incidents, inquiries, generated reports, and KPIs — giving teams an immediate pulse on operations without having to dig.

What makes the portal particularly powerful is its flexibility of context. The same views adapt seamlessly across three levels — a cross-company overview for leadership, a region-wise lens for regional managers, and an individual customer view for account-level visibility. Pair that with a structured ticketing system for issue tracking and an incidents layer for managing responses, and the result is a tool that doesn't just display data, it helps teams act on it.
